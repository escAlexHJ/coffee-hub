Homemade Flat White

Step 1: Gather your ingredients. You will need fresh coffee beans, milk, and water.

Step 2: Grind the coffee beans to a fine consistency using a coffee grinder.

Step 3: Brew a double shot of espresso using an espresso machine.

Step 4: Steam the milk until it is hot and has a velvety texture. Use a steam wand on your espresso machine.

Step 5: Pour the steamed milk over the espresso, holding back the foam with a spoon. Aim for a 1:3 ratio of espresso to milk.

Step 6: Serve your flat white coffee in a pre-warmed cup and enjoy!

Homemade Cappuccino

Step 1: Gather your ingredients. You will need fresh coffee beans, milk, and water.

Step 2: Grind the coffee beans to a fine consistency using a coffee grinder.

Step 3: Brew a double shot of espresso using an espresso machine.

Step 4: Steam the milk until it is hot and has a velvety texture with a good amount of foam. Use a steam wand on your espresso machine.

Step 5: Pour the steamed milk over the espresso, allowing the foam to flow out last to form a frothy top. Aim for a 1:1:1 ratio of espresso, steamed milk, and foam.

Step 6: Serve your cappuccino in a pre-warmed cup and enjoy!

Homemade Espresso

Step 1: Gather your ingredients. You will need fresh coffee beans and water.

Step 2: Grind the coffee beans to a fine consistency using a coffee grinder.

Step 3: Brew a single or double shot of espresso using an espresso machine.

Step 4: Serve your espresso in a pre-warmed cup and enjoy!

Homemade Americano

Step 1: Gather your ingredients. You will need fresh coffee beans and water.

Step 2: Grind the coffee beans to a fine consistency using a coffee grinder.

Step 3: Brew a single or double shot of espresso using an espresso machine.

Step 4: Add hot water to the brewed espresso to dilute it to your preferred strength. The typical ratio is 1:2 espresso to water.

Step 5: Serve your Americano in a pre-warmed cup and enjoy!

Homemade Latte

Step 1: Gather your ingredients. You will need fresh coffee beans, milk, and water.

Step 2: Grind the coffee beans to a fine consistency using a coffee grinder.

Step 3: Brew a single or double shot of espresso using an espresso machine.

Step 4: Steam the milk until it is hot and has a velvety texture. Use a steam wand on your espresso machine.

Step 6: Serve your latte in a pre-warmed cup and enjoy!

Homemade Mocha

Step 1: Gather your ingredients. You will need fresh coffee beans, milk, water, and chocolate syrup or cocoa powder.

Step 2: Grind the coffee beans to a fine consistency using a coffee grinder.

Step 3: Brew a single or double shot of espresso using an espresso machine.

Step 4: Steam the milk until it is hot and has a velvety texture. Use a steam wand on your espresso machine.

Step 5: Mix the brewed espresso with chocolate syrup or cocoa powder until well combined.

Step 6: Pour the steamed milk over the chocolate-espresso mixture, allowing a small amount of foam to form on top. The typical ratio is 1:3 espresso to milk.

Step 7: Serve your mocha in a pre-warmed cup and enjoy!

Homemade Frappe

Step 1: Gather your ingredients. You will need instant coffee, sugar, milk, water, and ice cubes.

Step 2: In a blender, combine 2 teaspoons of instant coffee, 1-2 teaspoons of sugar, 1/2 cup of milk, and 1/2 cup of water.

Step 3: Add a handful of ice cubes to the blender.

Step 4: Blend the mixture until smooth and frothy.

Step 5: Pour the frappe into a tall glass.

Step 6: Serve your frappe immediately and enjoy!

Homemade Black Coffee

Step 1: Gather your ingredients. You will need fresh coffee beans and water.

Step 2: Grind the coffee beans to a medium consistency using a coffee grinder.

Step 3: Boil water in a kettle.

Step 4: Place the ground coffee into a coffee maker or French press.

Step 5: Pour the hot water over the coffee grounds. If using a French press, let it steep for about 4 minutes, then press the plunger down slowly.

Step 6: Pour the brewed coffee into a cup.

Step 7: Serve your black coffee immediately and enjoy!
